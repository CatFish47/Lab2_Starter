2023/04/11 - ICC Meeting Minutes

The purpose of today's agenda is to examine the role that humans play in the grand scheme of the ICC's plans to take over the world. Click the audio file for an audio transcription of the meeting.


Minutes

Things to discuss:

  1. A cat's relationship to its human
  2. How they currently treat cats
  3. How cats want to be treated
  4. Action items

A cat's relationship to its human

As perceived by humans, cats are simply pets that the humans own, and as a result the humans will feel an obligation to take care of cats. Fortunately, they are oblivious to our intelligent selves and are none the wiser to our plan to dominate the world. Their homes offer shelter and warmth as we, the ICC, plan our next moves under the cover of night when the humans fall asleep. Truly immaculate.

How they currently treat cats

Cats currently are currently treated, for the most part, pretty well by humans. Despite our obvious dislike of prolong interaction with humans, they still somehow like us. However, some cats currently experience discomfort and dislike towards humans as a result of not being fed whenever the cats are hungry, due in part to how humans seem to want to control how much we eat. Fortunately, this is a problem that can be fixed by going over to the neighbors and meowing for food, as more times than not the neighbors will be more than happy to feed a cat.

How cats want to be treated

Aside from current accomodations provided by the average cat-"owning" human, some humans are still oblivious to how cats should be treated. Thus, below we have written a list of things that us cats want.

List of things cats want
  • Head scratches
  • Scratchable furniture
  • A comfortable litter box
  • All the food we want
  • Access to all rooms in the household at all times

Failure to comply will result in incessant meowing, as shown in the video below.

Action items

We have discussed the treatment of cats who live with humans and have come to the conclusion that cats are, for the most part, treated well. Under the circumstance that we feel as if we are not treated well enough, we will meow as loud as we can. As such, at the time there will be no action items.
